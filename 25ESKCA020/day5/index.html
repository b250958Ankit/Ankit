About The Event

What is PRAVAH?

PRAVAH means "flow" — the flow of ideas, energy, and talent that defines our annual fest.

PRAVAH 2026 brings together students, innovators, artists, and industry leaders for three days of learning, competition, and celebration. From hackathons to live performances, there's something for everyone.
